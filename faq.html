What is Fetch?

Fetch provides an easy-to-use service that pairs you with a carefully vetted, insured and bonded dog walker to give your best friend a walk to remember

General Questions


How does the walker gain access?


Answer: We're still in the early stages and have to use the traditional methods of either leaving a key or door code access. But that's why we requirethe dog owner to book a Meet & Greet to cover the arrangement of the access and the walk.


How do I know my dog has been walked (now and future integration)?


Answer: Right now, you'll recieve a text confirmation of the start and completion of your walk. But later on, we would love to include GPS tracking and other exciting features.


Will my dog be walked with the other dogs? (walk vs. dog park)


Answer: At the moment, we're only doing personal walks to perfect the user experience and gain better knowledge before taking on dog park visits. But if we did, you would have the final say if your comfortalbe having your dog around other dogs - guaranteed.


How do I pay my walker?


Answer: When you signup, you'll have to enter in your credit card info so we can bill you after each walk. Think of us as your dog walking assistant here to make your life easier.


Can I request a same day, on-demand walk? (now and future transparency)


Answer: Not at the moment but that's something we're looking at and wish your help we can make it possible. We cherish feedback and your happiness. If we fall short by any means, please email us at feedback@fetchawalker.com


How do I contact my walker?


Answer: After every booking, you'll recieve the contact info of your walker.
