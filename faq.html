What is Fetch?

Fetch provides an easy-to-use service that pairs you with a carefully vetted, insured and bonded dog walker to give your best friend a
walk to remember.

General Questions


Q: How does a Fetch walker gain access?


A: Right now the “traditional” way, hide-a-key, building manager, neighbor, door code access to name a few. But, be on the lookout for some great innovative solutions coming soon!


Q: How do I know my dog has been walked (now and future integration)?


A: You will receive a text confirmation at the beginning and ending of your dog’s outdoor adventure. The next integration of the app will include GPS tracking and a few other treats.


Q: Will my dog be walked with the other dogs? (walk vs. dog park)


A: For walks, it will be just your dog and their second best friend, for personalized jaunts…don’t be jealous! And, for dog parks, you pup will get into a party wagon with his or her four legged companions, for a social playtime.


Q: How do I pay my walker?


A: Fetch takes care of that all for you! When you signup, you will enter your credit card information securely through Stripe payments and we will charge after each walk has been completed.


Q: Can I request a same day, on-demand walk? (now and future transparency)


A: Not at the moment, but that is something we are looking to make happen! With you we can make it happen...keep using Fetch for all of your scheduled walks.

Q: How do I contact my walker?


A: After each booking, you will receive the contact info of your walker.

Walker Questions


Q: Why should I be a walker for Fetch?


A: We genuinely want to continue to improve upon the dog walking experience. For us, that starts with making your job as simple, efficient, and fun as possible. We understand your passion for dogs, and want to eliminate “the suck” that gets in the way.


Q: How do I become a Fetch walker?


A: We aim to provide better pay, flexible schedule, and less time driving around. By taking over the scheduling, billing, invoicing and marketing, it give you more time to play with happy dogs!


Q: What percent does Fetch take from my walk?


A: Right now, we are still figuring out what works and that is a process of what makes sense for both sides. Fetch puts it's walkers and dogs first, and will always aim for the highest of every standard.


Q: Does Fetch monitor my walk?


A: Right now our tracking is limited, but do have regular text checkups that relay to Fetch and the customer. Pending incorporating more features, this will be streamlined into the GPS.
