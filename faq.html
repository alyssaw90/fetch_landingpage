General Questions


Q: What does early access mean?


A: Currently we are in beta and testing things out to make sure your experience is perfect. In the meantime if you live in Seattle and would like to jump in with us now, join us by clicking the "early access" section.

Q: How does a Fetch walker get access?


A: Right now the "traditional" way, hide-a-key, building manager, neighbor, door code access to name a few. But, be on the lookout for some great innocative solutions coming soon!


Q: How do I know my dog has been walked?


A: You will receive a text confirmation at the beginning and ending of your dog’s outdoor adventure. The next integration of the app will include GPS tracking and a few other treats.


Q: Will my dog be walked with the other dogs?


A: For walks, it will be just your dog and their second best friend, for personalized jaunts…don’t be jealous! And, for dog parks, you pup will get into a party wagon with his or her four legged companions, for a social playtime.


Q: How do I pay my walker?


A: Fetch takes care of that all for you! When you signup, you will enter your credit card information secured with a 3rd party, and we will charge after each walk has been completed.


Q: Can I request a same day, on-demand walk?


A: Not at the moment, but that is something we are looking to make happen! With you we can make it happen...keep using Fetch for all of your scheduled walks.

Q: How do I contact my walker?


A: After each booking, you will receive the contact info of your walker.

Walker Questions


Q: Why should I walk with Fetch?


A: We aim to provide better pay, flexible schedule, and less time driving around. By taking over the scheduling, billing, invoicing, and marketing, it gives you more time to play with happy dogs!


Q: How do I become a Fetch walker?


A: Right now, we are fully stafed up, but contantly gorwing. But, you can visit this link and sign up. When we are looking for the next best fit, we will contact you and setup a coffee/beer to discuss.


Q: What percent does Fetch take from my walk?


A: Right now, we are still figuring out what works and that is a process of what makes sense for both sides. Fetch puts it's walkers and dogs first, and will always aim for the highest of every standard.


Q: Does Fetch monitor my walk?


A: Right now our tracking is limited, but do have regular text checkups that relay to Fetch and the customer. Pending incorporating more features, this will be streamlined into the GPS.
